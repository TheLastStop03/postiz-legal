Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11/14/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how this application (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects information when you connect your social media accounts and use the App to schedule or publish content. This App is operated by an individual and is used solely for personal content management and automation.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App only processes information that you explicitly authorize through the social media platforms you connect (such as TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, or X/Twitter). This may include:

Basic profile information (e.g., username, profile ID)

Access tokens provided by the platform to allow scheduled posting

Content you choose to upload or schedule (text, images, videos)

No other personal information is collected.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Your authorized information is used only for:

Connecting your social media account(s)

Scheduling or publishing content you choose

Managing authentication between you and the platforms

The App does not:

Sell or share your data with third parties

Use your data for advertising

Collect analytics

Track personal behavior

The App is entirely self-hosted and runs only on your local machine or environment.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device, inside your self-hosted environment.
No information is stored on external servers.

You maintain full control over all stored content, tokens, and settings.

If you delete the App or its associated data directory, all stored data will be permanently removed.

4. Third-Party Services

The App interacts only with the APIs of the platforms you intentionally connect, such as:

TikTok

YouTube

Instagram

Facebook

X/Twitter

These services may collect data under their own Privacy Policies.
You should review the privacy policies of each platform you connect.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies or tracking systems except those required for technical login purposes within the self-hosted environment.

6. Data Security

Because the App is fully self-hosted, you are responsible for securing your device, network, and local environment.

No external entity, including the App’s developer, has access to your data.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13.
It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
